Why Costa Rica

Discover Costa Rica

Costa Rica is home to over 30 national parks and protected areas covering approximately 25% of the country. With incredible biodiversity, stunning volcanoes, pristine beaches and lush rainforests, it's one of the most extraordinary destinations on the planet.

Pacific Coast Paradise

Costa Rica's Top Beaches

Golden sand, turquoise waters and endless paradise on the Pacific coast.

Most Photogenic

Playa Conchal

A unique beach made entirely of tiny crushed shells. Crystal-clear waters perfect for snorkeling.

Luxury

Playa Flamingo

Upscale beach with pink-hued sand, luxury marinas and world-class sportfishing.

Surfer's Paradise

Playa Tamarindo

Vibrant surf town with endless waves, beachfront restaurants and legendary sunsets.

Eco-Friendly

Playa Grande

Pristine, protected beach where leatherback sea turtles nest. Unspoiled natural beauty.

Family Favorite

Playa Las Catalinas

A charming beachfront village with calm waters, ideal for families and romantic escapes.

Legendary Surf

Roca Bruja

A dramatic rock formation rising from the ocean waves in Guanacaste. Known for powerful surf, stunning sunsets and a mystical atmosphere that gives it its name — Witch's Rock.

Turtle Nesting

Playa Avellanas

A stunning beach famous for its world-class surf breaks and as a key nesting site for Olive Ridley sea turtles. Every year, thousands of turtles arrive in mass nesting events known as 'arribadas' to lay their eggs on the dark sand shores.
